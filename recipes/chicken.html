Ham-burger



Description

A chicken burger the whole family enjoys! The onion can be finely chopped, we prefer grating it so as to hide it. I usually serve these with mashed potatoes and Caesar salad for a very comforting meal!

Chicken Burger Ingredients

  • Ground chicken
  • Bread crumbs
  • onions
  • Egg
  • Seasoning
  • Oil

Chicken Burgers Steps

  1. Mix the chicken, half of the crumbs, onion, egg, and seasonings in a bowl.
  2. Shape the mixture into oval-shaped patties.
  3. Coat the patties into the remaining bread crumbs.
  4. Cook the patties until they are deeply brown on both sides.
